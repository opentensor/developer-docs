Expand description

A range with a size that may be known at compile-time.

This may be:

  • A single usize index, e.g., 4
  • A left-open range std::ops::RangeTo, e.g., .. 4
  • A right-open range std::ops::RangeFrom, e.g., 4 ..
  • A full range std::ops::RangeFull, e.g., ..
