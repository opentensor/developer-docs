An Rank-One ConstraintSystem. Enforces constraints of the form ⟨a_i, z⟩ ⋅ ⟨b_i, z⟩ = ⟨c_i, z⟩, where a_i, b_i, and c_i are linear combinations over variables, and z is the concrete assignment to these variables.
